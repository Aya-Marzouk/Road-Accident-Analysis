# 🚗 Road Accident Analysis & Safety Insights

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides an end-to-end data analysis solution to identify patterns, causes,
and consequences of road accidents. Using a comprehensive dataset, it explores the impact
of weather conditions, road surface types, and light conditions on accident severity and casualties.

The project is built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**, featuring an interactive dashboard
with dynamic charts, KPI cards, and slicers for deep data exploration.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Source:** Road Accident Dataset (Excel)
- **Content:** Information on accident severity, weather conditions, road surface types,
light conditions, vehicle types, and casualties.

**Key metrics analyzed:**
- Total Casualties & Fatal / Serious / Slight breakdown
- Casualties by Vehicle Type (Car, Truck, Bus, Motorcycle, etc.)
- Accidents by Road Surface (Dry, Frost/Ice, Flood, Snow, Wet/Damp)
- Accidents by Light Conditions (Daylight, Darkness, etc.)
- Accidents by Severity (Fatal, Serious, Slight)
- Year-over-Year Accident Trends (2021 vs 2022)

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track total casualties with a clear Fatal / Serious / Slight KPI breakdown.
- Analyze year-over-year accident trends (2021 vs 2022).
- Identify the most dangerous road surface and light conditions.
- Segment casualties by vehicle type (Car, Motorcycle, Truck, Bus, etc.).
- Provide a data-driven basis for road safety improvements.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Data Processing:** Power Query (Excel)
- **Visualization:** Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, Slicers, KPI Cards, Donut Charts, Bar Charts)
- **Data Source:** Microsoft Excel (.xlsx)

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow
1. **Data Cleaning:** Handling missing values and standardizing road surface,
weather, and vehicle category fields using Power Query.
2. **Feature Engineering:** Creating casualty severity ratios and vehicle type groupings.
3. **Dashboard Development:** Building an interactive single-page Excel dashboard with:
   - KPI cards (Total, Fatal, Serious, Slight Casualties + Casualties by Car)
   - Bar charts for yearly trends and light condition analysis
   - Donut chart for accident severity distribution
   - Horizontal bar chart for road surface breakdown
   - Vehicle type panel with icons and counts
   - Date slicer and Road/Weather condition filter panel

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights
The Excel dashboard reveals critical safety patterns:

- **Severity Split:** The vast majority of casualties fall under the *Slight* category,
though Fatal and Serious cases remain a major concern at 289 and 1,968 respectively.
- **Year Trend:** Accident counts show a notable shift between 2021 and 2022,
visible in the Total Accidents by Year chart.
- **Road Surface:** Frost/Ice conditions stand out as a high-risk surface type,
appearing prominently in the Road Surface chart.
- **Light Conditions:** Darkness-related accidents represent a significant portion,
emphasizing the need for better street lighting and driver awareness.
- **Vehicle Type:** Cars account for the highest casualty share (325,921),
followed by motorcycles and trucks.

📌 **Key Insight:** Out of **16,305 total casualties**, the data highlights that
road surface quality and lighting conditions are among the strongest contributing factors to accident severity.
